Portfolio

Swiss Bank Analysis

Highlights of this project includes extracting data from the public database of swiss bank and transforming it into powerbi dashboards. Problem statement is “The impact of international transactions on swiss bank”.Parameters of this problem statement include types of currency, types of banking, types of transactions in the span of 10 years i.e from 2012 to 2022.

Dashboards include 4 pages where as the first page explains the impact of time on the international transactions With Bank type and transaction type as parameters. The second page includes the impact of currency on the transaction. Dollars have a high impact in both incoming and outgoing transactions.Third page specially focused on the Amount to understand if there is inclination or declination of amount with time. The fourth page includes a table with every single parameter used in the dashboards for better understanding.

User Friendly: I have included filters in every page at the top. So that the user can check the specific parameters, they wanna get the insights on. Also I have embedded powerbi dashboard directly in the portfolio so one can check the changes by clicking the graphs and it impacts all other graphs in a specific page and gives the insights on how one specific parameter impacts the remaining parameters.
